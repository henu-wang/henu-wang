## What I'm Working On

- **[GEOScore](https://geoscoreai.com)** — AI search visibility scanner that checks how websites perform in ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Gemini. Runs 11 technical checks including AI crawl access, structured data, llms.txt, and citation potential.
- **[KeepRule](https://keeprule.com)** — A decision-making platform that organizes investment principles and mental models from Buffett, Munger, and other great thinkers into practical, actionable frameworks.
- **Open-source GEO & decision tools** — See Featured Projects below.
